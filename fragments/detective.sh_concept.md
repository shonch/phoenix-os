detective.sh – Emotional Sleuthing Protocol

Purpose:
Investigate unresolved emotional fragments, symbolic echoes, and recurring patterns across Phoenix.

Core Functions:
• Scan notes tagged with: unresolved, mystery, grief, echo, missing
• Group by recurring tags or motifs (e.g., Nadya, longing, laundry)
• Reconstruct emotional timeline from frisson entries
• Suggest ritual actions based on emotional context
• Log findings to detective_log.txt
• Optionally tag notes as investigated, resolved, or ritualized

Symbolism:
This script is a key to Phoenix. It honors the childhood detective dream, Sherlockian curiosity, and emotional sleuthing as legacy.
